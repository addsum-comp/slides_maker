# Netherlands Job Market 2026 Template

## Template File

`template.pptx`

## Summary

A 13-slide, 16:9 analytical explainer deck about the 2026 Dutch job market. The visual system is structured, report-like, and advice-oriented: headline-first pages, clear sections, evidence blocks, practical takeaways, and policy/business framing.

Use this as a preset when the user wants an analytical deck that explains a market, industry, policy environment, job/career landscape, or decision context.

## Inspected Structure

- Slide size: 10.00 x 5.62 in.
- Slides: 13.
- Slide masters: 1.
- Layouts: 11.
- Built-in layouts include `Title Slide`, `Title and Content`, `Section Header`, `Two Content`, `Comparison`, `Title Only`, `Blank`, `Content with Caption`, and `Picture with Caption`.

The source deck begins with:

- `THE 2026 DUTCH JOB MARKET`
- `PART 1 · THE MACRO PICTURE`
- `WHERE THE JOBS ARE`
- `THE ENGLISH-SPEAKING REALITY`
- `PART 2 · The system: visas, money & politics`

These are content examples only. Do not preserve Dutch labour-market claims unless the user's new deck is actually about that topic and the source supports them.

## Use It For

- Market analysis and trend briefings.
- Career, talent, hiring, or labour-market explainers.
- Policy/business context decks.
- Structured advisory decks with audience-specific recommendations.
- Decks that need sectioning, evidence, and practical takeaways.

## Reuse Rules

- Keep the structured rhythm: clear title, section framing, evidence blocks, and actionable advice.
- Reuse the visual hierarchy and analytical tone, not the original Netherlands job-market content.
- Replace all facts, dates, market claims, country references, and recommendations with the user's actual source material.
- Maintain a strong distinction between sourced claims and forward-looking advice.
- If the user needs a highly visual travel/lifestyle deck, choose the Chengdu preset instead.
