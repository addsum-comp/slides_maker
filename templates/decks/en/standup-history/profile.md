# A Brief History of Stand-Up Template (English)

## Template File

`template.pptx`

## Summary

A 12-slide, 16:9 history / evolution narrative deck, built around "A Brief History of Stand-Up (1866 → today)", moving era by era: definition, the lecture circuit, vaudeville, the mic era, censorship, the golden age, the club boom, going global, streaming, China, and a closing. The visual system is chronicle-like, with era section headers. Chinese version: `zh/standup-history`.

Use this when the user wants a "how something evolved to today" narrative deck (history arc, industry evolution, cultural topic, timeline story).

## Inspected Structure

- Slide size: 10.00 x 5.62 in (16:9). Slides: 12. Layouts: 11.
- Fonts: Arial (body) + Arial Black (display).

## Use It For

- History arcs, brief-history explainers, timeline narratives.
- Cultural / industry "from origin to today" walkthroughs.

## Reuse Rules

- Keep the chronological rhythm: definition → eras → the present → closing; one era per slide.
- Reuse the era-section layout; replace with the user's topic and periods.
